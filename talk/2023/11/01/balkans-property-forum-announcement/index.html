    Keynote at Balkans Property Forum, Belgrade

    talk

    We are thrilled to announce that DataBrut CEO and founder, Ilija Lazarević will be delivering a keynote speech at the Hilton Hotel in Belgrade on November 30th. Join him for an engaging discussion on the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence in the Real Estate industry. He’ll explore the vital role of data and its potential to reshape Balkan Real Estate markets. Don’t miss this opportunity to gain valuable insights and unlock new horizons in your business.

    Save the date, and we look forward to seeing you there!
